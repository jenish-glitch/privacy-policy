Delete Your Account

Request permanent deletion of your Money Manager account and associated data

⚠️ Important Notice

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your financial data, transactions, budgets, and settings will be permanently removed from our servers.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your Money Manager account, please follow these steps:

  1. Send a deletion request email
    Email us at delete@moneymanager.app from the email address associated with your Money Manager account.
  2. Include required information
    In your email, include your full name, registered email address, and write "DELETE MY ACCOUNT" in the subject line.
  3. Verify your identity
    We may ask you to verify your identity by logging into the app one final time or providing additional verification.
  4. Confirmation
    You will receive a confirmation email within 48 hours acknowledging your deletion request.
  5. Account deletion
    Your account and data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of your request confirmation.

What Data Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be handled as specified:

Personal Information

DELETED

Name, email address, profile information

Financial Data

DELETED

All transactions, budgets, categories, and financial records

App Settings

DELETED

Preferences, notifications, and customization settings

Usage Analytics

DELETED

App usage patterns and interaction data

Legal Records

RETAINED

Basic transaction logs kept for 7 years for legal compliance

Support Tickets

RETAINED

Customer support communications kept for 2 years

Data Retention Policy

Immediate Deletion (0-30 days)

Your account will be deactivated immediately, and most personal data will be deleted within 30 days of your request.

Legal Compliance Retention (7 years)

Due to financial regulations, we must retain minimal transaction records (amounts, dates, categories) for 7 years. This data is anonymized and cannot be linked back to you.

Support Records (2 years)

Customer support communications are retained for 2 years to help resolve any potential disputes or technical issues.

Before You Delete

Consider These Alternatives

If you're concerned about privacy or want to take a break, consider these options instead of permanent deletion:

  • Export your data: Download all your financial data before deletion
  • Disable notifications: Turn off all app notifications in settings
  • Log out: Simply log out and uninstall the app temporarily
  • Contact support: Reach out if you're having technical issues we can help resolve
